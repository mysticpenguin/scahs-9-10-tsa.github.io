Benefits and Uses

Many people believe that A.I can be harmful to their business, however this is a false notion. A survey done by Sage Group has revealed that small companies spend around 120 days a year doing administrative tasks (Sharma). Artificial Intelligence could save a large majority of that time by performing those tasks for you. As a result, these small companies could spend more time focusing on their customers and improving their business. This is also more cost-effective as well. Now companies do not have to pay for employees as the A.I. can do tasks faster and more efficiently.
