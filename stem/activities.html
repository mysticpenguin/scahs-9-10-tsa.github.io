State High offers a wide variety of STEM activites besides TSA:

  • Science Olympiad: Small teams of 2-3 people each compete in 23 different events.
  • Math Club: Students can compete in various math related events.
  • HOSA (Health Occupations Students Of America): The State High Health Professions Team
  • Science Bowl: Teams compete to answer science related trivia questions.
  • Ocean Bowl: Teams compete to answer ocean related trivia questions.
