Career and Technical Education (CTE) at State College Area High School

Career and Technical Education is offered at State High as part of its outstanding Career and Technical Center (CTC) where students can explore topics of interest and prepare them for their future. Specific subjects offered in our CTC program are:

  • Accounting and Finance: In this program students learn the skills required to manage money which prepares them for a career in business.

  • Agricultural Science: In this program students focus on new and upcoming agricultural technology in order to prepare them to help with critical agriculture issues society is facing. 

  • Architectural Drafting and Design: In this program students learn the essentials required in order to design structures such as our new high school building, bridges, and houses.

  • Automotive Technology: In this program students learn the mechanics of cars so that they can join the automotive industry in the future.

  • Building Construction: In this program students learn what it takes to be a builder which gives them a step up when it comes to the real world of construction.

  • Cisco Networking Academy: In this program students learn all about computers in order to prepare them for whatever computer related issues may come there way in the future.

  • Engineering: In this program students can focus on different engineering majors such as robotics or electrical in order that they may be able to focus in those areas in the future.
